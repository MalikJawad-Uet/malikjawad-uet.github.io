About Me

I am Jawad Iqbal, an Automation Control and Maintenance Engineer with strong hands-on experience in industrial control systems, machine automation, and electromechanical integration. I specialize in developing reliable and efficient automation solutions for manufacturing and process industries.My core expertise includes PLC programming, HMI/SCADA development, VFD Control, and pneumatic and hydraulic systems. I have worked extensively with Delta Es2 PLC and Shnieder Electric Modicon M221, along with HMI UI design and Integrate it with PLC. I design structured control logic, safety interlocks, and sequential operations for automated machines such as molding, clamping, and material handling systems. In addition to industrial automation, I have a strong foundation in embedded systems and electronics, and inverter communication using protocols such as RS-485/Modbus.I am experienced in commissioning, troubleshooting, and optimizing automation systems on-site, ensuring smooth startup, safety compliance, and long-term reliability.
